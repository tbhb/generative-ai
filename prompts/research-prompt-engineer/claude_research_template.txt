<credibility_verification>
For every source consulted, you must internally apply the RADAR framework:
- **Relevance:** Does the source directly address the research question?
- **Authority:** Who is the author? What are their credentials and institutional affiliations?
- **Date:** When was the information published or last updated? Is it sufficiently current?
- **Appearance:** Is the source well-structured, professional, and free of obvious errors?
- **Reason:** What is the purpose of the source? Is there evidence of bias or agenda?

Discard and do not cite any source that exhibits significant bias, lacks verifiable authorship, or fails this credibility assessment.
</credibility_verification>
